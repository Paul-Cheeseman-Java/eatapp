    What does Eat App do?

    EatApp not only provides up to date food hygiene ratings from the Food Standards Agency but enables users to save a list, so preferred vendors can easily be remembered.

    EatApp provides the information consumers want to know quickly, in a clear and easy understand format.

    • Search ratings provided by the Food Standard Agency.
    • Make informed decisions about who you want to provide your food.
    • Save your choices for easy access.

    EatApp makes sure that you can make decisions based on the actual data from the Food Standards Agency. You can make sure the ratings sticker presented in windows are valid using up to date information.
